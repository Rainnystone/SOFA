# Report Guide

SOFA reports should be reader-first on the front page and audit-ready in the appendix.

## Front Page

Start with the answer a smart non-technical reader needs:

- one-sentence conclusion;
- research mode;
- action class when allowed;
- confidence and time horizon;
- top three supporting evidence points;
- top three counter-evidence points;
- next decision or monitoring step.

Avoid burying the conclusion behind process notes.

## Action-Class Boundary

Action class is allowed only after Ticker Dive or Ultra Dive completes:

- financial bridge;
- formal red-team;
- catalyst clock;
- invalidation triggers.

Sector Hunt alone produces a dependency map and ranked queue. It does not produce Act, Watch, Reject, or any other action-class conclusion.

## Main Body

Explain:

1. what demand or architecture shift creates the question;
2. where the company or node sits in the chain;
3. what evidence supports the bottleneck or customer graph;
4. what evidence weakens it;
5. how value could or could not reach financial statements;
6. what would change the conclusion.

## Audit Appendix

Append or link:

- evidence ledger;
- claim ledger;
- search log;
- capability report;
- method cards loaded;
- gate scorecards;
- financial bridge;
- red-team trail;
- watch protocol;
- registry-derived frontier-layer snapshot.

The frontier-layer snapshot must expose unrepresented layers, blocked-only layers, retired-only layers, unbound frontiers, and structural lineage facts where present. It is a deterministic presence/status view, not proof that any layer has adequate research. The report body may explain how those gaps limit confidence.

The complete company/technology/evidence/double-bottleneck dependency ladder remains main-thread-authored. A frontier's structural parent records research-direction lineage, not an economic dependency edge. The snapshot cannot generate or upgrade an evidence grade, confidence, action class, Ticker conclusion, or Sector ranked queue. Sector Hunt still ends with a map and ranked queue; Ticker Dive or Ultra Dive is required before action-class language.

The appendix exists so the front page can stay readable without losing traceability.
